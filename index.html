Visit Dublin

Dublin, a city that's as intimate as a village and as friendly as an Irish pub. Framed by mountains, centred on a river and edged by a beautiful bay

Top three activities to do in dublin

Go for a picnic in the local park

The dublin Park is perfect for picnic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Attend a show or a concert

The dublin Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Play sports at the Lilleborg field

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
